Province overview

This page gives an overview of the current state of public transport categorized for each province. It will provide more insight into several topics, e.g. population and busstops per inhabitant.

Population

Above graphs show how the population of the Netherlands is divided among the different provinces. It is clearly visible from the choropleth on the left that most people live in the west. The piechart on the right shows what percentage of the total population this is and how they compare to each other.

Population per km2

The two images above support our claim that most of the population is concentrated in the west. The three provinces that stand out are: Zuid-Holland, Noord-Holland and Utrecht. These three provinces combined are known as the Randstad. It contains the four biggest cities of the Netherlands. We will be comparing these provinces with the other, less densily populated, provinces.
